Welcome to The Haukai Restaurant

Traditional Māori Kai, sourced right here from our beautiful Bay of Islands

We are a newly established restaurant in the beautiful Bay of Islands. We provide a traditional and authentic dining experience with the fusion of Māori and Pākeha kai (food)

Owned by a couple of kiwis, born and raised, who wanted to incorporate the indeginous culture of Māori, intertwined with classic local New Zealand dishes.

Our kitchen uses only the freshest produce sourced from the local area which we know you will love!

We also provide a wide selection of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages to cater for every family member.

The Haukai Restaurant looks forward having you dining with us!
